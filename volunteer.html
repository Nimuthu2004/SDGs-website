Volunteer Programs

Beach Cleanup Volunteer

Join local beach cleanups to remove plastic waste and debris from coastlines, protecting marine life and preventing pollution.

Duration weeks: 2-12 weeks

Age: 16+

Coral Reef Restoration

Assist in coral planting and monitoring reef health to restore damaged coral ecosystems and promote marine biodiversity.

Duration weeks: 1-15 weeks

Age: 18+ (2 weeks)

Sea Turtle Conservation

Help conserve endangered sea turtles by assisting with feeding, cleaning, and monitoring nesting sites.

Duration weeks: 2-8 weeks

Age: 16+

Sustainable Fishing Education

Work with fishing communities to promote sustainable fishing practices and reduce bycatch.

Duration weeks: 4-12 weeks

Age: 18+
